# 故宫红金（gugong-red · 东方新中式）— Compact

> Neo-Oriental heritage system founded on the deep oxblood of a palace wall (#9A2B2B), edged in bright imperial gold and grounded by dai-blue roof shadow. Heavy Song-serif titles stand vertically like an unrolled handscroll while a cinnabar seal breaks a hairline gold lattice frame as the signing stamp. Sharp zero-radius blocks, wide silk-paper whitespace, and high-contrast Didot-style numerals carry archival serials. Built for museums, cultural heritage, premium heritage craft, and archival collections. Ceremonial, restrained, unmistakably imperial, never a template. Signed HeiGe-Design.

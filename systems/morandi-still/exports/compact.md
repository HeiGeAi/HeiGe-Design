# 莫兰迪静物（morandi-still · 有机自然）— Compact

> Morandi Still — a hushed, high-grey system for homeware, ceramics and slow lifestyle brands. Anchored by a single dusty slate blue (#55676B) and warmed by clay-rose and dusty-sage accents over a plaster ground, it treats every layout as a still-life: objects grouped asymmetrically on one shared groundline, one directional light wash, one long soft shadow, and vast quiet negative space. A soft Fraunces serif carries the calm; an airy humanist sans breathes underneath. Restraint is the entire point, not a compromise.

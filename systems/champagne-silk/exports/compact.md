# 香槟丝绸（champagne-silk · 奢侈高定）— Compact

> A couture-beauty system in champagne gold over warm ivory, lit like light sliding across silk. Champagne (#e8d9be) is the material; a deep couture rouge (#8e3b44) is the one drop of color spent on action, and a soft silk rouge (#d98a8a) tints the sheen. Ultra-thin Cormorant serif fades up over layered soft-light gradients; wide-tracked Jost small-caps stand beneath at attention. UI runs on ivory, the deck goes to warm espresso noir. Signature: a silk sheen behind the hero, a full-bleed rouge numeral dividing the deck.

**Theme：** light

# 炉边（hearth-story · 温暖人文）— Compact

> Hearth-Story is a warm humanist system for nonprofits, community groups and social enterprises — the light of a room lit by a low fire. A deep ember primary glows against warm oat paper and sand surfaces while charred-walnut ink carries long, readable stories. Fraunces serif sets emotional display headlines and Source Sans keeps body copy human and calm. The bright ember accent never touches text; it survives only as glow, brushed underlines and spark marks. The signature is a hand-brushed ember signoff and a before-and-after story turn that warms cool grief into firelight. Tactile, restrained, never corporate.

**Theme：** light

## 用前先读（5 条铁律）

1. 主色 `#ac4914` 只给关键 CTA 与强调，克制使用。
2. 复现完整 DESIGN.md 里 `## Signature Moment` 写死的签名时刻。
3. 中文字体栈末尾必带系统兜底，中文不用日文字体渲染。
4. 不碰紫蓝俗套渐变，不做居中卡片三件套。
5. 交付前对照 `## Do's and Don'ts` 自查，跑 `heige-design lint hearth-story` 确认 0 error。

# 丰收（harvest-warm · 有机自然）— Compact

> Croftmark is a woodcut-warm system for organic agriculture, farm-to-table and market brands. Earth-brown ink presses onto a kraft-cream field, wheat-gold stamps the harvest, deep leaf-green lines the margins. Fraunces cuts old seed-catalogue serifs against rounded Nunito, so headlines read carved and copy reads hand-warmed. The signature is the perforated stamp edge and hand-set produce label: every card and cover looks like a crate stencil pressed onto paper, never a stock SaaS panel.

**Theme：** light
